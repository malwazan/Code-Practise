Hi everyone, the above given is my official TOEFL SCORE. I know this is not a great score, but it would be enough to get you into top colleges because anything above 100 is considered a very good score in TOEFL. I prepared for about two weeks so diligently and got this score. Many people get even 114 or 117 or even 120 with merely two days of preparation time. But it conspicuously reflects their level of English. They might be used to the practice of reading newspapers, articles, and novels from their childhood or during their undergraduate studies. If you are not one among them, definitely my preparation format can help you, because I am similar to you and it took only 2 weeks for me to reach this score.

I wrote TOEFL last year in August. That was my first time and honestly, I didn’t prepare for it. I had the GRE on August 9th and the TOEFL was on August 12th. I thought I could score like those intellect people so I just saw the format and attempted the test. My score was just 89.

So, I understood I was not one among them. Then after a year, I decided to take the TOEFL again.

I allotted for about 2 weeks and 4 days nearly. The first 4 days went away by searching for trustful TOEFL resources.

If you register on their website, they would mail you a video every week which you can follow and study. It really helps. But you should do this only if you have more than 2 weeks. If you don’t have more than 2 weeks, either watch their YouTube videos or enroll in their TOEFL mastery course which costs 50 dollars. But I have made it uncomplicated for you, the below link will give you access to all their videos, strategies and practice questions. Trust me, this is going to make you achieve your dream score. The only thing is you have to review these videos so many times, to remember these. Watching these one time would not aid you.

Finally, the speaking and the writing sections are those which you cannot know how you are performing. Of course, there are websites where you can pay and get your essays scored and even your speaking responses. But I actually didn’t prefer to spend money on those. So I don’t know about that. Official TOEFL GUIDE helped me so much in this part. There comes a CD along with the book. You have to take the book, turn to the speaking section. There are four speaking questions and for each question, you would be provided with sample responses. These are the treat to you. You must listen to those carefully and should be able to discern the difference between a medium-level response and a high-level response. There will only be subtle differences but that improves your points to a great extent. Believe me, I thought I collapsed my speaking section. I was stammering so much during my response, but I just got the points right. So it is significant to know what a high-level response is. Another thing which you should do is, record your responses and hear them. Do the same question 3–4 times, this may sound lame, but you will be able to see gradual improvement.

Writing section is the section that I struggled a lot. I was very bad at punctuation and grammar. Integrated essays don’t require you to jot down your ideas, so it will be facile. But independent essay require you to brainstorm ideas. Each day I wrote two independent essay. I used grammarly.com to help me. After writing the essay, post it in the website, it would give you a score and also will notify all the grammar mistakes, spelling errors and a lot more. Don’t get disheartened by those scores. I got only 50 out of 100 even the day before my exam which engendered 25 in writing. If you are able to take more than 50, you would be able to get more than 25. Implementing templates given in the notefull, also assist you to get a better score because for every essay there are certain sentences that are the same. If you practice writing the same in every essay eventually you will not make any mistakes in those sentences in the exam.

This is all that I did in the 2 weeks and I can assure you 100+ if you take serious efforts on these resources and prepare accordingly. There will always be people who tell you, you need these many hours to improve even a single point in TOEFL. But it is not at all true, every person differs, and every person’s mental ability, confidence level and all the traits differ. I was the person who took 89, and I was the person who took 109 in just two weeks. Believe yourself, you can do if I can :)

Thanks for your time. Best of luck to everyone who is about to take the test. If you like my preparation format and if you feel it helped you, give me a clap. BYE. :)
